##### The ingredients needed to make Mushroom Risotto:

1. Make ready 1 cup arborio rice
1. Take 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
1. Take 1 small onion diced
1. Get 2 cloves garlic
1. Make ready 1 cup wine
1. Take  chicken or vegetable stock
1. Get 1 tsp thyme
1. Make ready  salt and pepper
1. Make ready  parmeson cheese
1. Take  parsley




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Mushroom Risotto:

1. Dice onion and add to pot with olive oil. Season with thyme. Add mushrooms and season with salt and pepper
1. Add 1 cup of rice. Let cook for a minute. Add the wine first and stir. Once the liquid is absorbed add one ladle of stock and stir.
1. When the rice is al dente grate cheese and stir into mixture. You can add one more ladle of stock and then season with parsley
