##### The ingredients needed to make Quick Ruben Crescent Bake:

1. Prepare 2 8 oz Pillsbury Crescent Pastry Sheets
1. Take 1 lb Sliced Swiss Cheese
1. Make ready 1 1/4 lb Sliced Deli Corned Beef
1. Make ready 14 oz Sauerkraut rinsed &amp; drained
1. Make ready 2/3 cup Thousand Island Salad Dressing
1. Make ready 1 Egg White




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Quick Ruben Crescent Bake:

1. Unroll 1 pastry &amp; press into a greased 13x9x2 in baking pan.
1. Bake at 375° for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
1. Place half of the cheese on top of the pastry.
1. Top with all of the corned beef.
1. Mix Salad dressing &amp; sauerkraut in a bowl then spread over the corned beef.
1. Top with remaining cheese.
1. Press the 2nd pastry across the top.
1. Quickly whisk the egg white in a small bowl then brush over the pastry.
1. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown. Let stand 5 minutes.
